# General Questions

Frequently asked questions about IMP Money.

## What is IMP Money?

IMP Money is a decentralized DeFi yield protocol on BNB Smart Chain. It generates yield through automated liquidity provision on PancakeSwap V3 and offers a 21-level referral system.

## Is IMP Money safe?

IMP Money has been:
- ✅ Audited by CertiK
- ✅ Deployed with renounced ownership
- ✅ Operating on verified, open-source contracts
- ✅ Run by a UK registered company

However, all DeFi carries risks. Only invest what you can afford to lose.

## How do I earn money?

Two ways:

1. **Daily ROI (0.7%)** - Deposit USDT and earn 0.7% daily on your deposit
2. **Referral Commissions (14%)** - Earn when people in your 21-level network deposit

## What is the minimum deposit?

**$10 USDT** is the minimum deposit amount.

## Is there a maximum deposit?

No maximum. Deposit as much as you want.

## What blockchain is IMP on?

**BNB Smart Chain (BSC)** - Chain ID 56

## What wallet do I need?

Any Web3 wallet that supports BSC:
- MetaMask
- Trust Wallet
- Coinbase Wallet
- WalletConnect compatible wallets

## Do I need BNB?

Yes, a small amount (~$2-5) for transaction fees (gas).

## Is this a Ponzi scheme?

No. The yield comes from real DeFi trading fees on PancakeSwap V3:

1. User deposits go into liquidity pools
2. Trading volume generates fees
3. Fees are distributed as ROI

The referral system is additional incentive, not the source of yield.

IMP Money operates as a DeFi protocol. Users interact directly with smart contracts on the blockchain. The UK company provides operational support and development.

## Can I lose money?

Yes, risks include:
- Smart contract bugs (mitigated by audits)
- Market conditions affecting LP yields
- Stablecoin depeg events
- User error (wrong addresses, etc.)

Only invest what you can afford to lose.
