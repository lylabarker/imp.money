# Claiming Profits

Your profits accrue daily at 0.7% of your deposited amount. This guide explains how to claim them.

## How Profits Accrue

- ROI: **0.7% daily** on your deposit
- Calculated: Every block (~3 seconds)
- Claimable: **Anytime** (no waiting period)

### Example Accrual

| Time Since Deposit | $1,000 Deposit Earnings |
|-------------------|------------------------|
| 1 day | $7.00 |
| 7 days | $49.00 |
| 14 days | $98.00 |
| 21 days | $147.00 |

## Claiming Your Profits

### Step 1: Go to Dashboard

Navigate to [imp.money](https://imp.money) with your wallet connected.

### Step 2: View Claimable Amount

Look for the "Claimable" or "Yield" section showing your available profits.

### Step 3: Click Claim

1. Click the **"Claim"** button
2. Review the transaction
3. Confirm in your wallet
4. Wait for confirmation

### Step 4: Receive USDT

Profits are sent directly to your wallet as **USDT**.

## Claiming Strategies

### Strategy 1: Daily Claims
- Claim every day
- Good for covering expenses
- More gas fees over time

### Strategy 2: Weekly Claims
- Claim once per week
- Balance of liquidity and efficiency
- ~7 days of profits per claim

### Strategy 3: Full Cycle Claims
- Wait 21 days
- Claim everything at once
- Most gas-efficient

### Strategy 4: Compound (Reinvest)
- Claim and re-deposit
- Grows your principal
- Maximizes long-term returns

## Gas Costs

- Each claim transaction costs ~$0.10-0.20 in BNB
- Batching claims is more gas-efficient
- Claims are processed in a single transaction

## FAQ

### Can I claim while my deposit is locked?
**Yes!** Profits are always claimable. Only your principal is locked for 21 days.

### Is there a minimum claim amount?
**No minimum**, but consider gas costs. Claiming $0.50 with $0.15 gas may not be optimal.

### How often should I claim?
Balance your cash flow needs with gas efficiency. Weekly is a good middle ground.

### Do unclaimed profits keep growing?
**Yes!** Profits continue to accrue whether you claim or not.
