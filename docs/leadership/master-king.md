# Master King Rank

The highest rank in IMP Money - reserved for legendary network builders.

## Master King Requirements

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Kings Stars in Network | 10 |
| Team Members | Typically 25,000+ |
| Business Volume | Typically $5M+ |

## The Ultimate Bonus

Master King receives:
- **Standard Kings Star bonus**: 7% differential
- **Plus additional +0.5%** on ALL network ROI

This 0.5% is applied universally, regardless of downline rank.

## How +0.5% Universal Works

### Regular Kings Star
```
Your rank: Kings Star (7%)
Downline: Star 5 (5%)
You earn: 7% - 5% = 2%
```

### Master King
```
Your rank: Master King (7% + 0.5%)
Downline: Star 5 (5%)
You earn: 7% - 5% + 0.5% = 2.5%
```

The +0.5% applies even when ranks are equal:

```
Your rank: Master King
Downline: Kings Star (7%)
Regular diff: 7% - 7% = 0%
Master King bonus: +0.5%
You earn: 0.5%
```

## FAQ

### How do I track progress?
Dashboard shows Kings Stars in your network.

### Do indirect Kings Stars count?
Yes! Kings Stars anywhere in your downline count.

### What if a Kings Star demotes?
They must maintain requirements. If they drop, you may lose the count.

### Is there anything above Master King?
Master King is currently the highest rank.
