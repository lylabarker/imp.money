## How Requirements Are Calculated

### Team Size
**Total members in your downline** across all 100 levels.

Includes:
- Direct referrals (L1)
- Their referrals (L2)
- And so on, up to 100 levels

### Business Volume
**Cumulative deposits** from your entire team.

Example:
- 50 team members Ã— $500 average = $25,000 volume

### Direct Referrals
**Active L1 referrals** who have deposits.
