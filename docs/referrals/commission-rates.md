## Commission Groups

### Direct Team (L1-L6): 7.75%
These are your closest connections - people you refer directly and their immediate circles.

### Extended Team (L7-L12): 3%
Your network expands through duplication.

### Deep Network (L13-L19): 1.75%
Leaders building leaders building leaders.

### Max Depth Bonus (L20-L21): 1.5%
Rewards for truly deep network building.

## How Commissions Are Paid

1. **Someone deposits** through your network
2. **Smart contract calculates** your level from them
3. **Commission is paid instantly** in USDT
4. **Funds go directly** to your wallet

No waiting. No minimum threshold. Instant payment.

## Example Calculation

**Scenario**: You have all 21 levels unlocked. 

Someone 15 levels deep deposits $1,000:

- Your level from them: 15
- Level 15 commission: 0.25%
- **Your commission: $2.50 USDT**

**Network Total**:
If 100 people across your network deposit $500 average:
- Total volume: $50,000
- If average across levels = ~0.7% (weighted)
- **Total commission: ~$350 USDT**
