# Getting Your Referral Link

Share your unique referral link to build your network and earn commissions.

## Finding Your Referral Link

### Step 1: Connect Wallet
1. Go to [imp.money](https://imp.money)
2. Click "Connect Wallet"
3. Approve the connection

### Step 2: Make a Deposit
You need at least one active deposit to generate referral links.

### Step 3: Navigate to Network
1. Click on "Network & Referrals" in the menu
2. Or click the referral icon on your dashboard

### Step 4: Copy Your Link

Your unique referral link will look like:
```
https://imp.money?ref=0xYourWalletAddress
```

Click the copy button to copy it to clipboard.

4. You receive commission instantly

## Link Tracking

On your Network page, you can see:
- Number of direct referrals (L1)
- Total network size
- Commissions earned
- Team activity

## Multiple Links

Your referral link is tied to your wallet address. You have one link per wallet.

If you want separate tracking:
- Use different wallets for different campaigns
- Each wallet has its own referral link

## FAQ

### Do I need to deposit to refer?
Yes, you need at least one active deposit to be eligible for referral commissions.

### Can I change my referrer?
No, once you deposit with a referrer, that relationship is permanent.

### What if someone deposits without a referral link?
They'll be assigned to the protocol's default referrer. You can't claim them retroactively.

### Can I refer myself with another wallet?
Technically possible, but you'd need deposits in both wallets to earn, making it inefficient.
