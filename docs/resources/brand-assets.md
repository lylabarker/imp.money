# Brand Assets

Official IMP Money brand assets for partners, affiliates, and media.

## Logo

### Primary Logo
The IMP Money logo consists of:
- Lightning bolt icon in mint green (#00FF94)
- "IMP" wordmark in white

### Logo Variations
- **Full color** - For dark backgrounds
- **White** - For colored backgrounds
- **Black** - For light backgrounds

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "I" around all sides.

## Colors

### Primary Colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Mint Green | `#00FF94` | Primary accent, CTAs |
| Black | `#0A0A0A` | Background |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Text |

### Secondary Colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Gold | `#FFD700` | Leadership, highlights |
| Light Gray | `rgba(255,255,255,0.6)` | Secondary text |
| Border | `rgba(255,255,255,0.1)` | Borders, dividers |

## Typography

### Primary Font
**Inter** - Available from Google Fonts

- Headings: Inter Bold (700)
- Body: Inter Regular (400)
- Emphasis: Inter Semi-Bold (600)

## Usage Guidelines

### Do's ✅
- Use official logos without modification
- Maintain color integrity
- Keep clear space around logo
- Use on brand-consistent backgrounds

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't stretch or distort the logo
- Don't change logo colors
- Don't add effects (shadows, gradients)
- Don't use on busy backgrounds
