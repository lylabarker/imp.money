# Yield Calculator

Calculate your potential earnings with IMP Money.

## Basic Calculator

### Formula
```
Daily Earnings = Deposit × 0.7%
Cycle Earnings (21 days) = Deposit × 14.7%
Yearly Earnings = Deposit × 255% (approx, without compounding)
```
